Organized the root justfile's recipes into `[group(...)]` categories along two axes -- project (mngr, minds) by activity (install, dev, test, build, plus a minds-only ops group for pool/slice/deploy operator recipes) -- so `just` with no arguments now lists recipes under headings like `[mngr test]`, `[minds dev]`, and `[minds ops]`, making it easier to find the right command. The previous coarse `[group("minds")]` tags were refined into the specific categories; no recipe behavior changed.
