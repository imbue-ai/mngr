Added `specs/agent-usage-plugins/spec.md`: a design spec for extending `mngr usage` cost/usage tracking beyond Claude to the OpenCode, pi, and Codex harnesses. The spec generalizes the usage event schema to report raw token counts (with the reader deriving and provenance-flagging cost via a canonical pricing table), keeps dollars as the cross-harness comparable unit, and lays out three thin per-harness writer plugins. Antigravity and the Claude-subagent-proxy are documented as out of scope.
