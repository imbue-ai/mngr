Inspiration deeplinks (`minds://create?git_url=...`) now land on a dedicated Create from Inspiration page instead of the plain create form. The page is a numbered vertical stepper that shows one step at a time: finishing a step collapses it to a summary of what you picked (the options you didn't choose disappear) and reveals the next; a completed step can be clicked to go back and change it. Step 1 asks whether to create a new workspace from the Inspiration or add it to an existing one.

Adding to an existing workspace walks through: (2) copy the `/use-inspiration <repo>` message -- the workspace picker stays hidden until you copy -- then (3) pick one of your workspaces to open and paste it into that workspace's chat (stopped local workspaces are restarted on the way in, matching the landing page).

Creating a new workspace walks through: (2) cloud vs. local -- no advanced settings -- then (3) the remaining confirmation: the account, the Inspiration repository shown as plain read-only text, and a required "I trust this Inspiration" acknowledgment (Inspirations are community content, not approved or verified by Imbue). The acknowledgment shows in red and the "Create from Inspiration" button stays grayed out and disabled until it is checked. The workspace is otherwise configured exactly like a normal preset create.
