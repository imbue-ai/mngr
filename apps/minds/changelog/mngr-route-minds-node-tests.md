Routed `apps/minds/scripts/build_test.py` to a Node-equipped CI job. Registered a new `minds_node_required` pytest marker (in `apps/minds/conftest.py`, alongside the other minds markers) and applied it to the test, replacing the previous `skipif(node is None)` guard.
