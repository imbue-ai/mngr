Hardened two flaky creation unit tests (`test_start_creation_api_key_ai_does_not_mint_litellm_key` and `test_start_creation_subscription_ai_does_not_mint_litellm_key`) against heavy offload contention by adding `@pytest.mark.flaky` as a retry backstop. The existing `@pytest.mark.timeout(30)` already raises the pytest kill-timer above the global 10s default, and `_wait_until_finished` keeps its 30s default poll deadline. No product behavior change.

Fixed the `propagate_changes` dev-loop script on macOS: its step-1 `vendor/mngr/` rsync syncs the monorepo into `TEMPLATE_DIR/vendor/mngr/`, where `TEMPLATE_DIR` lives under the monorepo's `.external_worktrees/`. It relied on the `.gitignore` rule `**/.external_worktrees/` to skip that dir, but macOS's stock rsync (openrsync, "2.6.9 compatible") does not support `**` globs, so it copied the destination into itself and raced its own `--delete` (exit 23). Added an explicit `--exclude=.external_worktrees` (matching the same `just minds-start` fix). Also updated `apps/minds/docs/vendor-mngr-sync.md` to document the explicit exclude.
