Made the `agent_creator_test` creation tests robust against heavy parallel CI load. The shared `_wait_until_finished` poll deadline was 10s while the tests already carried a 30s `pytest.mark.timeout`; under load the real creation work (fresh ConcurrencyGroups + the recording http-server fixture) occasionally took longer than 10s and tripped the internal deadline before the pytest budget. The poll deadline now sits just under the 30s pytest-timeout, which stays the hard backstop. `test_start_creation_api_key_ai_without_key_fails_with_clear_message` (which shares the same `_wait_until_finished` helper) now also carries the 30s `pytest.mark.timeout`, so the raised 25s poll deadline stays just under it rather than being pre-empted by the global 10s timeout. Test-only change.
