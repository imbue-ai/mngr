Made the `agent_creator_test` creation tests robust against heavy parallel CI load. The shared `_wait_until_finished` poll deadline was 10s while the tests already carried a 30s `pytest.mark.timeout`; under load the real creation work (fresh ConcurrencyGroups + the recording http-server fixture) occasionally took longer than 10s and tripped the internal deadline before the pytest budget. The poll deadline now sits just under the 30s pytest-timeout, which stays the hard backstop. Test-only change.
