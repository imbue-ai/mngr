Added `/modal/sandboxes` routes (create, exec, tunnels, get/set tags, list, terminate) that provision Modal sandboxes in the connector's own Modal workspace on the authenticated caller's behalf. Because the connector runs as a Modal function it has ambient control-plane access, so callers need no Modal credentials of their own. Every sandbox is tagged with its owner and all reads/mutations are scoped to that owner; the routes reuse the same SuperTokens bearer + paid-account auth as the host-lease routes. This backs the "Modal (experimental)" compute option (`ModalMode.PROXIED`).
