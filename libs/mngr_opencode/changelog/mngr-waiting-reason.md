Clarified in the README why a `waiting_reason` of `PERMISSIONS` is not feasible for opencode: its only extension point is the in-process event bus, and permissions are an upfront config policy (`allow`/`deny`/`ask`) rather than an interactive event, so opencode emits no permission-request signal when an `ask` policy blocks. Only `END_OF_TURN` (from the `active` marker) could be surfaced.
