Split the loading page's stylesheet into `_LOADING_PAGE_BODY_CSS` (the full-viewport body-centering) and `LOADING_PAGE_CARD_CSS` (the card-scoped rules), and added a `render_loading_card()` helper that renders just the `.card` fragment. This lets a consumer place the loading card inside its own document shell (the minds desktop app now renders the workspace-recovery page under its titlebar) while the proxy loader is byte-for-byte unchanged (`LOADING_PAGE_CSS` is still both halves).
