Changed how the single-discovery-producer invariant is enforced. `mngr latchkey forward` now accepts `--observe-exit-alongside-pid PID`, which it forwards to its `mngr observe` discovery child as `--exit-alongside-pid` so the producer dies when that PID exits. Only the producer is tied to the PID -- the detached forward and its shared gateway and reverse tunnels are not, so they still survive an embedder restart.

This replaces the previous approach (added in the duplicate-producer fix) of hunting the process table on every startup and reaping other `mngr latchkey forward` processes bound to the same latchkey directory. That startup reaping (`_reap_duplicate_forwards` and its cmdline/`--latchkey-directory` scanning, descendant capture, and PID-reuse guards) has been removed: with the producer leashed to the embedder, a prior instance's producer no longer outlives the embedder, so there is nothing to reap on the next launch. The fix also moves earlier -- the producer dies promptly when the embedder exits or crashes, instead of lingering until the next launch sweeps it.
