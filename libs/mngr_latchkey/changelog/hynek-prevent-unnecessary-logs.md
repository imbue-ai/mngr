`mngr latchkey forward` no longer logs "Discovery error from ..." warnings for providers that are disabled in the active config (e.g. a provider turned off in the minds providers panel). These errors are written to the shared discovery log by other mngr processes (such as `mngr list`) and were previously echoed at warning level into the forward's `events.jsonl`, spamming it about providers the user intentionally disabled. Such errors are now dropped to trace; discovery errors for enabled providers are still surfaced at warning.
