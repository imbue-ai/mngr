`mngr message` now declines fast, with a clear error, when a Claude agent is sitting on an interactive selection panel (opened by a TUI-local slash command such as `/model`, `/status`, `/config`, or `/login`) instead of pasting the message into the panel's own input field. Previously the message was appended to the panel and the send failed after a long wait with a confusing diagnostic. The panel is detected by its Escape-to-dismiss footer (`Esc to cancel` / `Esc to clear`), which is present in these panels but absent from the ready and mid-response states, so ready and busy agents are unaffected. The error names the condition and points to `mngr connect <agent>` to resolve it.
