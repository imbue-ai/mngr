The common-transcript converter's event-conversion logic moved out of an inline `python3` heredoc in `common_transcript.sh` into a standalone `common_transcript_convert.py` (provisioned alongside the shell script), so it is type-checked, linted, and unit-tested directly. Malformed raw-transcript lines and unreadable existing-output lines are now logged at warning level rather than being dropped silently. A transcript line whose `message` is `null` (rather than an object) is now treated as an empty message instead of aborting the entire conversion run.
