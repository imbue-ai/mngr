`scripts/release.py` now refuses to cut a release when there are unconsolidated entries in `changelog/`, since those would otherwise be omitted from the version's release notes. The gate offers to invoke the `changelog-consolidation` schedule on demand (the same one that normally runs nightly), so the human can land its PR and then re-run the release. The plugin-disable args used around `mngr schedule` invocations now live in `scripts/trigger_changelog_consolidation.py` and are shared by `scripts/setup_changelog_agent.sh`.
