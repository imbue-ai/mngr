Bump per-test timeout to 60s on `test_destroy_via_stdin` and `test_destroy_multiple_agents` in `libs/mngr/imbue/mngr/cli/test_destroy.py`. Both tests create two `sleep` agents, wait for their tmux sessions, run a parallel destroy, and wait for cleanup, which routinely lands at or over the global 10s pytest-timeout under modal-offload contention. `@pytest.mark.flaky` alone was not enough headroom -- `test_destroy_via_stdin` exhausted all 5 retries in CI. Matches the existing pattern on `test_destroy_transfer_none_keeps_shared_worktree`.
